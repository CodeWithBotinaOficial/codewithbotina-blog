# About CodeWithBotina

CodeWithBotina is a developer community and knowledge hub created to share practical, real-world software experience. It exists to help engineers ship better systems, learn faster, and make confident technical decisions without being locked into a single framework or toolchain.

## About Diego Alejandro Botina

I’m Diego Alejandro Botina, a software developer and educator. I build products, APIs, and platforms that prioritize reliability, performance, and maintainability. My work spans a broad stack and adapts to the problem at hand: frontend, backend, data, infrastructure, and the workflows that connect them.

## Mission

To publish clear, actionable content that helps developers solve real problems, improve their craft, and grow their careers. The focus is on practical engineering outcomes over hype: what works, why it works, and how to apply it.

## Vision

To build a Spanish-speaking community where high-quality software knowledge is accessible, modern, and grounded in real practice. A place where developers can learn across technologies, make better decisions, and contribute back to the ecosystem.

## What We Build Here

- Deep dives into software architecture, performance, and scalability
- Practical tutorials and build notes from real projects
- Best practices for APIs, databases, and system design
- Tool-agnostic guidance that emphasizes long-term engineering quality

## Core Principles

- Clarity over complexity
- Pragmatism over trends
- Quality over shortcuts
- Teaching as a path to mastery
