CodeWithBotina is a developer community and knowledge hub focused on practical, real-world software engineering. The goal is to help developers ship better systems, learn faster, and make confident decisions without being locked into a single framework or toolchain.

## Diego Alejandro Botina

I’m Diego Alejandro Botina, a software developer and educator. I design and build products, APIs, and platforms that prioritize reliability, performance, and maintainability. My work spans frontend, backend, data, and infrastructure, adapting to the problem rather than a fixed stack.

## Mission

Publish clear, actionable content that helps developers solve real problems, improve their craft, and grow their careers. The focus is on practical engineering outcomes: what works, why it works, and how to apply it.

## Vision

Build a Spanish-speaking community where high-quality software knowledge is accessible, modern, and grounded in real practice. A place where developers learn across technologies, make better decisions, and contribute back to the ecosystem.

## What You’ll Find Here

- Deep dives into software architecture, performance, and scalability
- Practical tutorials and build notes from real projects
- Best practices for APIs, databases, and system design
- Tool-agnostic guidance that emphasizes long-term engineering quality

## Core Principles

- Pragmatism over trends
