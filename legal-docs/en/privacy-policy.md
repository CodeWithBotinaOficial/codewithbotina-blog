# CodeWithBotina Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction

CodeWithBotina ("we", "our" or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

**Contact:** support@codewithbotina.com  
**Website:** https://codewithbotina.com

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Authentication Data (Google OAuth)

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

- **Email address**: To identify your account
- **Full name**: To personalize your experience
- **Profile photo**: To display in your user profile
- **Google user ID**: To link your account

### 2.2 Usage Data

- **IP address**: For security and fraud prevention
- **Browser and device type**: For optimization
- **Pages visited and time on site**: For analytics
- **Comments and reactions**: Content you publish

### 2.3 Cookies

We use cookies to:
- Keep your session active
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze site usage

Authentication cookies currently use these retention periods:
- Access token cookie: 1 hour
- Refresh token cookie: 7 days
- OAuth PKCE cookie: 10 minutes
- First-party auth state hint: 7 days

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes

- **Authentication**: Verify your identity
- **Personalization**: Show your name and photo in comments
- **Communication**: Notify you about replies to your comments
- **Security**: Prevent unauthorized access and fraud
- **Service improvement**: Analyze usage to improve the experience

### 3.2 We Do Not Sell Your Data

- We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties
- We do **not** use your data for targeted advertising
- We do **not** share your data unless legally required

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Location

Your data is stored in:
- **Supabase** (database): Servers in the United States
- **Cloudflare Pages** (frontend): Global CDN
- **Deno Deploy** (backend): Globally distributed servers

### 4.2 Security Measures

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure storage of tokens
- Restricted access to sensitive data
- Regular security reviews

### 4.3 Data Retention

- **Account data**: While your account is active
- **Comments**: Indefinitely (or until you delete them)
- **Access logs**: 90 days
